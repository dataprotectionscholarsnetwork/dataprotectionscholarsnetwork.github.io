Online Seminar with Prof. Dr Orla Lynskey and Prof. Dr Lee Andrew Bygrave on Publishing on Data Protection: Monographs and Generalist Journals

November 10, 2025

DPSN Community Join Us for an Insightful Online Seminar!

Mark your calendars for December 4th from 9:00 AM to 10:00 AM CET!

The DPSN is delighted to invite you to a special seminar featuring two of our very own Members of the DPSN Steering Committee: Dr. Orla Lynskey from the London School of Economics and Prof. Lee Andrew Bygrave from the University of Oslo.

Discover valuable insights on publishing in generalist journals and monographs focused on data protection. Whether you’re a budding researcher or an experienced academic, this session promises to enrich your understanding and provide practical advice.
