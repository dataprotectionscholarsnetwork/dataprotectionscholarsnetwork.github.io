Join DPSN and LSTS for Monthly Digital Coffees: Latest Data Protection Judgments from CJEU on 25 September 2024

September 11, 2024

If you are interested in staying up-to-date on the latest judgments of the EU Court of Justice, do not miss the monthly digital coffee event co-organised by DPSN and LSTS VUB.

Join us for an informal exchange of ideas and insights as we discuss the most interesting 1-2 judgments on the agenda at the CJEU in the past month. This is an excellent opportunity to stay informed about the latest developments in data protection.

Whether you are an academic, a legal professional, a data protection officer, or simply interested in this topic, we welcome you to join us for this exciting event.

Everyone is welcome to join and contribute their insights!
