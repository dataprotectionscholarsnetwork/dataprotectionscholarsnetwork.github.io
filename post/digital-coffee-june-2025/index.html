Join DPSN and LSTS for Monthly Digital Coffees: Latest Data Protection Judgments from CJEU on 4 June 2025

May 16, 2025

Join DPSN and LSTS for our next Digital Coffee: Exploring the latest CJEU judgments on data protection – 4 June 2025.

If you are interested in staying up to date on the latest judgments of the EU Court of Justice in the data protection field, do not miss the Monthly Digital Coffees co-organised by DPSN and LSTS-VUB. Join us for an informal, open, and interactive exchange of ideas as we delve into some of the most thought-provoking CJEU judgments from the past months.

This isn’t a lecture — it’s a space to exchange views, discuss, ask questions, and think together about where the data protection law is heading. Whether you’re an academic, legal professional, data protection officer, or simply curious about the field, your voice matters. Come share your perspectives and be part of the conversation.

In this session, we will discuss CJEU Judgment in joined cases C‑313/23, C‑316/23 and C‑332/23 (Inspectorate at the Supreme Judicial Council, Bulgaria) on the GDPR and judicial authorisations to lift banking secrecy, particularly concerning the disclosure of data related to the bank accounts of judges and public prosecutors.

The case will be introduced by Dr. Juraj Sajfert (post-doctoral researcher at the VUB).

We are meeting on Wednesday, June 4th, at 1.30 pm CET via MS Teams.

Everyone is welcome to join and contribute their insights!
